What is a full-stack developer?
A full-stack developer builds both the front-end and back-end of websites and applications, handling databases, servers, and user interfaces.
What is a full-stack salary?
Full-stack developer salaries vary by experience and location, typically ranging from ₹6–12 LPA for freshers to ₹15–30+ LPA for seniors in India.
What are the skills of a full-stack developer?
Skills include HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, databases, Git, problem-solving, and communication skills.
